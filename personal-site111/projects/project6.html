Multi-Perception Wearable Device

Category: Wearable Computing · Tools: DSP, WM8978, RTOS · Year: 2024
This wearable device integrates multi-modal perception modules to support speech data collection and analysis from autistic individuals. It features high-fidelity audio capture and real-time processing.
The system utilizes the WM8978 audio codec, an RTOS-based firmware architecture, and DSP pipelines for noise reduction, segmentation, and emotional feature extraction.
