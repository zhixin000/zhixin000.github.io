Sun-Grinding Machine

Category: Kinetic Installation · Tools: BLDC, AS5600, Modbus · Year: 2023
This kinetic installation uses the metaphor of “grinding the sun,” exploring the relationship between light, labor, and feedback. As participants rotate the large wheel, real-time sensor data drives dynamic LED animations.
A 2804 BLDC motor and AS5600 magnetic encoder measure rotation speed and position. Data is transmitted via Modbus RTU to a control card that updates the LED behavior through the HJ212 protocol.
