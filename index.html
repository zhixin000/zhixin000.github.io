About

Hi! I'm Zhixin. I am an atypical engineer — someone who loves microcontrollers as much as painting. My work explores the spaces between design, technology, and human emotion. I blend embedded systems, interaction design, and visual storytelling to create systems that feel intuitive, gentle, and meaningful.
